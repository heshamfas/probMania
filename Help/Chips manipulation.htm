  • To find out which belongs to who,

    The chips will fall as the following:

    • The chips of the two players  at the top left and the top right  will fall as the position of players standing next to the second  and the third base dealers consecutively.  
    • The chips of the two players  at the middle left and the middle right  will fall as the position of players standing three from the second  and the third base dealers consecutively.  
    • The chips of the two players  at the bottom left and the bottom right  will fall as the position of players standing next to stick left and right consecutively. 

  • Refer to the Tutorial section of the game to learn all about the players positions.

  • The following is an explanation to how to use the chips feature in PropMania.

 

Getting Chip information

 

  • Position the mouse on the chip, a popup text tool will appear showing how much and what type the bet is.

Expanding and retracting the stacks of chips

 

  • Click with the right mouse button on any chip to expand the bet and see it's contents.

 

  • While the mouse is still down, drag away from the chips to keep the stacks in their expanded position.

 

  • click the right mouse button on the chips again to make them return to their original position

 

If the bet  consists of other numbers, the a highlighter will flash at the bottom of the bet showing the stack of chips clicked on and any other related stacks of chips.

     Dragging the chips

  • Expand the stack of chips by right clicking
  • Drag away to keep the expanded status
  • Click on any chip in the stack to drag it and all the chips above it in same stack.

 
There is no need to drag the chips anywhere because they are perfectly positioned where they suppose to be, but if you like to try it for fun then :
  • To drag one chip at the time:
  1. Click with the left mouse button on any chip
  2. With the mouse button still down drag the chip any where you like same way as dragging icons on your computer desktop
  • To drag a whole stack of chips:
  1.  Expand the stack of chips by right clicking on the stack.
  2.  Drag away from the stack to keep it on its expanded position as explained above.
  3. Click your left button mouse at the bottom of the stack  and drag away.
  4. Drag portion of the stack or individual chips.

 

  • To send the chips back to their original position:
  1. Click your right mouse button on any of chips that belong to that stack .
  2. Release the mouse button.
  3. The chips will return back to where they belong.
