Getting a new roll

  • To start the game or get a new roll simply click on the start button
  • All the players will disappear then they will appear randomly one at the time
  • Each player will announce his or her  bets
  • After all the players announce their bets a rolling dice will show up and stop on a natural Number (2,3,7,11 or 12) 
  • To Find out what kind of bet each player has just position your mouse on that player
  • A pop up text tool will show you how much and what type of bet the player has.
