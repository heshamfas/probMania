The Prop Calculator

  •       The prop calculator is a cool tool to get  calculate proposition bets .
  •       With the Prop calculator you can  calculate the most complex combination of bets.
  •        The prop calculator will correct your bet amount to the closest valid bet amount giving you the difference as change.

 

  •         How to Use the Prop Calculator

    Using the prop calculator can be summarized into the following steps. 

    1. Selecting a bet type.
    2. Selecting the roll.
    3. Entering  the bet amount and calculating the bet.

     

  •           Calculating complex combinations.

 

 

 

  1. Selecting a bet type:

  • Click on the prop Bet button

  • With the mouse button still down drag down to the desired bet type

  • Release the mouse button, the selected type of bet will appear on your calculator screen.

 

  1. Selecting the roll.

  • Click on the Dice Roll button

  • With the mouse button still down drag down to the desired Dice Roll

  • Release the mouse button, the selected Dice Roll will appear on your calculator screen.

  1. Entering  the bet amount and calculating the bet.

  • Using the numbered buttons as a calculator buttons enter the bet amount
  • The clicked numbers will appear on the calculator screen as you click.
  • If you make a mistake click the back button <-- to correct
  • Click on the calculate button to get the result
  • If the bet is illegitimate bet amount the calculator will find 
the closest legitimate  amount then will give the rest as change.

 

 

  •           Calculating complex  bet combinations.

       With the Prop Calculator you can find the answer to the most complicated proposition bet.

       Example:

  •       A player has  :

    • $100 Horn

    • $99 Hi Lo Yo (33 each).

    • $635 Horn Hi twelve

    • The roll is twelve

Getting the Answer:

  1. Calculate the $100 Horn using the instruction mentioned above ($675)

  2. Click on the MS button to store the result in the memory and replace any other contents in it.

  3. Calculate the $99 Three way craps using the calculation instructions.($924)

  4. Click the M+ button to  add the result to the previously stored results

  5. Calculate the $635 Horn Hi Twelve Using the same instructions.($7239)

  6. Click the M+ button to add the result to the previously  stored results.

  7. Now that you finished calculating all the bets Press the MR  button to retrieve the final result.($8838)

 

Using the same approach you can get the results of the most complicated bets you can ever imagine.
