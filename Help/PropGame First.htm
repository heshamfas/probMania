PropGame 

Prop Game is the most important section of the game that will

get you acquainted with the complexity of the game.

The object of this section is to present more complex bet construction, before they happen on your game.

Even though there are much more complex combinations, often training with this section will remove the fear of facing them.

  • Note that the players positions are not the same as the positions of a life game.

  • The chips will fall as the following:

    • The chips of the two players  at the top left and the top right  will fall as the position of players standing next to the second  and the third base dealers consecutively.  
    • The chips of the two players  at the middle left and the middle right  will fall as the position of players standing three from the second  and the third base dealers consecutively.  
    • The chips of the two players  at the bottom left and the bottom right  will fall as the position of players standing next to stick left and right consecutively. 

  • Refer to the Tutorial section of the game to learn all about the players positions.
