The Tutorial section

The tutorial section of the game provide you with comprehensive explanation of the proposition bets.

  • The tutorial  contains two parts:

    • First part is for teaching how to position the bets for the players
    • Second part is for get the dealer acquainted with the many types of proposition bets
  • you have to go through the first part to get to the second part

 

 

  • First Part of the tutorial
    • Use the next and back buttons to navigate through this part of the game

 

First part of the tutorial section of the game is to teach you how to position the chips for different players
 

  • The second part of the tutorial

    • The next and back buttons are to navigate through this part of the game.
    • The Menu button is to go back to the introduction section of the game.
    • The Practice Button is to take you to the practice by bet section of the game.
    • Position your mouse on any chip  to find out what the bet is.
    • Click on the chip to find out how much the bet pays.

    • The Dice Functions:

      • Click on a chip to find out how much that bet pays.
      • Click on the dice to get a different roll.
      • Go back to the bet to see the new payoff according to the new roll.
      • Repeat the process with different types of bets.

       

Second part of the tutorial section of the game is to teach you about the different types of proposition bets and how much they pay.
