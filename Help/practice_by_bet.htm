Practice By Bet

The practice by bet section is for dealers who would like to concentrate on specific type of bet. 

 
  • Press the bet kind button to bring down a list of the most common types of bets
  • With the mouse button still pressed drag down to the desired kind of bet
  • When you see the desired bet highlighted release  the mouse button

  • The user have chosen to practice the "AceDeuce Yo" type of bet.
  • Set the level to the desired complexity..
  • Click the start button to generate the bet.
  • Three answers will appear on the left pane.
  • Click on the answer you think is correct.
  • A response will appear indicating correct or incorrect answer on the frame above the answers frames.
